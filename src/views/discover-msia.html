States & Federal Territories in Malaysia

The Must-Go

Kellie's Castle

A mysterious, unfinished mansion blending various architectural styles, rumored to be haunted.

Taiping Lake Gardens

Malaysia's oldest public garden, renowned for its serene lakes and century-old rain trees.

Menara Condong Teluk Intan

A historic, leaning tower from 1885 in Perak, Malaysia, often compared to the famous Pisa.

Gua Tempurung

A vast limestone cave in Perak, Malaysia, famous for its ancient rock formations and archaeological finds.

The Must-Eat

Restoran New Hollywood

A popular Ipoh eatery known for its diverse hawker food, including Chee Cheong Fun and Ipoh white coffee.

Nasi Ganja Yong Suan

A legendary Ipoh spot famous for its flavorful Nasi Kandar with a unique 'ganja' curry.

Kong Heng Restaurant

A nostalgic Ipoh restaurant offering classic local dishes like Char Kway Teow and Beef Noodles.

Larut Matang Hawker Centre

A popular food hub in Taiping offering a wide variety of local delicacies, including Char Kway Teow and Prawn Mee.

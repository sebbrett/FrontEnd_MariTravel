Malaysia Truly Asia

Places to explore

Explore the beauty and culture of Malaysia's diverse states, where every corner offers a unique experience filled with stunning landscapes, vibrant cities, and warm hospitality.

Perak

A state with a rich tin mining history, famous for the royal town of Kuala Kangsar.

Malacca

A state known for its well-preserved colonial architecture and as the birthplace of the Malay Sultanate.

Johor

A southern state famous for its bustling cities, pristine islands, and lush rainforests.

Penang

An island state celebrated for its vibrant street food culture, heritage sites, and beautiful beaches.

Selangor

A modern and dynamic state, home to Malaysia's capital, Kuala Lumpur, and numerous cultural landmarks.

Sarawak

A vast state on Borneo, renowned for its rich indigenous culture, dense rainforests, and majestic rivers.
